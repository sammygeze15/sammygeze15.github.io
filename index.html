THE PROBLEM

We realised that these small clothes selling boutiques are limited to the market provided by the people that live around them locally hence they dont get as much profit

We also found out that these boutiques usually are not informed of the fashion trends today hence they lose market from the present generation

Solution

We decided to create an application that allows these small boutiques to get exposed to the various locations around the country

We are also making a way in which these boutiques can learn more about what kind of fashion is currently trending to attract market from from the current generation which will in turn increase their market
